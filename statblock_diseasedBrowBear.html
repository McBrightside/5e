Keen Smell.

The bear has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

Charge.

If the Rabid Brown Bear moves at least 20 ft. straight toward a target and then hits it with an attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or else it becomes diseased (Rabies). Whenever the creature with rabies long rests it accumulates one stack of the disease. Each stack of rabies reduces a creature's maximum hit points by 1 hit point, and their Constitution, Dexterity, and Strength saving throws are reduced -1. A creature can have no more than five stacks of rabies.
